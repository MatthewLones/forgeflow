PRODUCT
-------
NovaBridge is an AI-powered code migration platform that automatically converts legacy enterprise codebases (COBOL, Fortran, legacy Java) to modern languages (Python, Go, Rust) while preserving business logic and generating comprehensive test suites.

Key differentiator: proprietary "semantic graph" technology that understands business intent, not just syntax. Competing solutions achieve ~60% accuracy on complex migrations; NovaBridge consistently hits 92%+ with human-in-the-loop review.

PRODUCT FEATURES
- Automated codebase analysis and dependency mapping
- Incremental migration with rollback capability
- Generated test suites with 85%+ coverage
- CI/CD integration (GitHub, GitLab, Jenkins)
- Compliance-aware migrations (SOX, HIPAA, PCI-DSS)
- Self-hosted or cloud deployment options
