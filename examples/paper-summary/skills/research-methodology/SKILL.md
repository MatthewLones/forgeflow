# Research Methodology

## Overview

This skill provides domain knowledge for evaluating the methodology section of academic research papers. It covers common study designs, statistical methods, and criteria for assessing methodological rigor.

## How to Use This Skill

When analyzing a research paper's methodology:

1. Identify the study design (RCT, cohort, case-control, cross-sectional, etc.)
2. Evaluate the sample size and selection criteria
3. Assess the statistical methods used
4. Check for potential biases (selection, measurement, confounding)
5. Evaluate the validity of conclusions given the methodology

## Key Criteria

- **Internal validity**: Was the study designed to minimize bias?
- **External validity**: Are the results generalizable?
- **Statistical power**: Was the sample size adequate?
- **Reproducibility**: Could the study be replicated from the description?
